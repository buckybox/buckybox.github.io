About Team Contact

We started Bucky Box to make it easy to run a successful local food delivery business.

Since 2011 Bucky Box has powered all manner of local food deliveries including vegetable box schemes, artisan breadmakers, community supported agriculture farming, food co-operatives, and recipe bag subscriptions. And everyday we're working hard to support more types of operations.

2011

April

Prototype Created

Will Lau fresh from helping friends deliver local food to doors discovers a big lack of software, he talks to compulsive do-gooder, Joshua Vial, and the two assemble a rag team of volunteers who head to a cabin in the woods for 12 days to build a prototype system.

August

Starting Up

Joshua Vial raises some seed funding between friends and colleges and incorporates Bucky Box as a startup within the Enspiral Network, Joshua's other main occupation.

December

Second Round Funding

Will Lau provides a second funding round and moves to Wellington, NZ to lead the startup.

2012

April

Bucky Box visits India

By following the magic, Bucky Box sends a team of four to India to study the food system for six weeks. They bring back a soulful long-term purpose and vision.

May

Private Testing

Bucky Box starts private testing with local food delivery businesses in Australia and New Zealand.

August

Bucky Box wins R&D Grant

Bucky Box is awarded a government R&D grant from the Ministry of Science and Innovation (Callaghan Innovation).

November

Public Launch

With great joy and merriment Bucky Box is launched to the general public.

December

International Banking Support

With some Jordan Carter's brilliance, a scripting engine is rolled out allowing Bucky Box to set up bank transactions from banks worldwide easily.

2013

January

Bucky Box Runs in Three Countries

Three months on from public availability, Bucky Box powers local food operations in three countries. A collective sign of relief is heard from people in New Zealand, Australia and the UK.

February

Local Food Challenge

Sam Rye opens the $500k local food startup challenge to support the creation of new local food businesses.

June

Email Integration

Cédric rolls out the Bucky Box emailer, making customer engagement much easier allowing for templated emails right from the customer database.

November

Bucky Box API

By popular demand, Josh D. opens up Bucky Box to third party developers via an API.
