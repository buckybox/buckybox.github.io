How do I select my plan?
We'll do this for you. We'll automatically charge you according to the plan which works out the least expensive for you each month.
How does the delivery fee work?
You pay a small fee per box delivered. This fee is calculated as a small percentage of the delivery or the capped value, whichever is smaller.
How do I pay?
Our system sends out a PayPal invoice each month which can be paid by credit card. We will migrate onto credit card or direct debit in the near future depending on your location.
Other languages?
The web store, customer dashboard and emails are available in English, Dutch, French, German, Italian and Portuguese. We welcome translations into other languages by the community.
